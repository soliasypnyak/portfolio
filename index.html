Who I Am?

Hi! I am Solia, ukrainian artist and a 5th year student at the Lviv College of Decorative and Applied Arts named after Ivan Trush.

What I Do?

I am specialized in oil painting, monumental painting, murals and stained glass restoration. Based in Lviv, Ukraine. I work as an artist and teacher at the Yellow Art Studio.

My Education

Graduated from the Lviv College of Decorative and Applied Arts named after I. Trush in 2024. Received a professional junior bachelor's degree in "Fine Arts, Decorative Arts, Restoration".

I am a teacher

I teach children and adults various drawing techniques: oil painting, watercolor, graphics. I organize master classes, creative camps and creative events.
